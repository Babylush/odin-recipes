Sex In Driveway Cocktail Recipe

Description

A cool looking cocktail that can be easily made at home. Bring paradise to your humble estate with this boozy recipe.

Simple ingredients and simple steps, I hope you enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 1 Ounce Peach Schnapps
  • 1 Ounce Blue Curacao
  • 2 Ounces Vodka
  • Sprite or any lime flavored soda

Steps

  1. Fill a tall glass with ice.
  2. Pour in schnapps, blue curacao, and vodka
  3. Top off with sprite and stir
  4. Enjoy!
